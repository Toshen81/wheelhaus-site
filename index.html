About Us

At WheelHaus Rentals, we believe transportation is more than just getting from point A to point B — it’s a pathway to freedom, opportunity, and stability.

Founded in Greenville, South Carolina, our mission is simple: to help hardworking people stay on the road and in control of their income. Whether you're a dedicated DoorDash driver or navigating life’s transitions, we understand how critical reliable transportation is to your success.

Rooted in a passion for service and family, we created this company to be flexible, affordable, and people-centered. Our rentals are designed with gig workers in mind — giving you the freedom to earn without the stress of traditional car ownership or credit checks.

We know what it’s like to juggle family, work, and responsibility. That’s why WheelHaus exists — to support your hustle, your goals, and your peace of mind.

Let us help you keep moving forward.
